FAQ

HT vs HTL—what’s the difference?

HT emphasizes core bench skills; HTL adds validation, troubleshooting depth, and molecular-adjacent skills.

Do I need lab access?

No. Modules include practice and printable drills; bench drills are optional.

How long is a module?

30–60 minutes of focused study, plus practice drill time.
