FAQ

Answers to common questions from HT/HTL candidates.

Is this aligned to the ASCP HT/HTL exam?

Yes. Modules follow the publicly available content areas (Fixation, Processing, Embedding/Microtomy, Staining, and Lab Operations). We’re not affiliated with ASCP.

What do I get in the free preview?

Access to the Staining mini-module: overview notes (PDF) and a short practice quiz. More free samples are coming.

When will paid plans be available?

We’re finishing core modules now. Join the waitlist below—early members get launch pricing.

Do I need prior experience?

No. Lessons start with fundamentals and build to HTL-level depth. Short quizzes help identify gaps.

Will there be live sessions?

Yes—weekly review/Q&A sessions are planned for the full program. Replays will be available.

Can I use this at work for CE or training?

Some labs may accept it for internal training; CE accreditation is not available yet. Ask your supervisor for local policies.

I built Free HTL Guide to make certification prep clear, structured, and practical. My background spans routine histology and molecular pathology at the bench—fixation and processing through embedding, microtomy, special stains, and quality control. Lessons focus on what the HT/HTL exam actually tests, with checklists and troubleshooting tips you can apply at work.

Focus areas

  • Fixation parameters & tissue preservation
  • Embedding & microtomy technique
  • Special stains & QC (H&E, PAS, GMS, AFB, trichrome, iron, retic)
  • Artifact recognition & corrective actions
  • Exam strategy & time management

Short videos, printable one-pagers, and targeted quizzes. Weekly live reviews are coming—so you get feedback and structure.
