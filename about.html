Our mission

Built by histotechs, for histotechs.

Help histology professionals and students build durable bench skills while achieving top scores on the ASCP HT and HTL exams.

Our story

We’re instructors and working histotechs who needed prep that matched real lab life. We built a curriculum that maps 1:1 to the ASCP outline and wrapped it in stain‑centric visuals, calculators, and adaptive practice.

It started as a few lessons for our cohort—and became a full platform as more techs asked for the same training.

Not affiliated with or endorsed by ASCP BOC. We align to their publicly available content outlines to help you prepare effectively.

Frequently asked questions

Are you affiliated with ASCP?

No. We are independent educators who align to the ASCP BOC HT/HTL content outline.

How long is the program?

Standard 8 weeks (or 12‑week paced). You can go faster/slower; progress saves.

What’s included?

Modules 1–5, Stain Atlas, calculators, quizzes/mocks, downloads, and updates.

Do you offer HT vs HTL tracks?

Yes. HTL content layers additional depth (IHC/ISH/enzymes, validation, regulatory).

Team training?

Yes — group dashboards, assignment dates, and completion reporting.
